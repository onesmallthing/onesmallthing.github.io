Easy Vegetarian Lentil and Boiled Egg Soup Recipe

Authors
  • Name
    Fany Carolina Vargas
    Mastodon

I felt a cold starting to come on and wanted to make a soup that was easy to make and would help soothe my throat. It turned out pretty good, so I thought I'd share the recipe. This recipe makes two servings. I'm estimating the measurements, so you may need to adjust them to your taste.

Ingredients

  • Lentils, 1 cup
  • Large Carrots, 3
  • Baby Bella Mushrooms, 4
  • Fresh Mini Sweet Peppers, 4
  • Garlic, 2 cloves
  • Water, 4 cups
  • 1 onion
  • Salt, Pepper, Red Pepper Flakes, and Ground Cumin to taste
  • 2 boiled eggs (optional - we had some leftover boiled eggs in the fridge)
  • Fresh Cilantro

Pots and Pans

  • Saucepan

Instructions

  1. Chop the carrots, peppers, and onion into small pieces.
  2. Mince the garlic.
  3. Add the lentils, onion, garlic, and water to a pot.
  4. Add salt, pepper, red pepper flakes, and ground cumin to taste.
  5. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer. Let simmer for about 25 minutes.
  6. Add the carrots, peppers, and mushrooms. Let boil for 10 minutes more.
  7. Serve with a boiled egg and fresh cilantro.
